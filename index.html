02 / Approach

How I Work

◎

Diagnose before designing

I start with a content audit or problem framing — not a blank doc. Understanding what exists, what's broken, and what the user actually needs comes before any writing.

⬡

Systems over one-offs

I design content frameworks, models, and patterns that scale. Any solution I build should work for the tenth use case, not just the first.

◈

Decisions, documented

I write content briefs, decision logs, and rationale docs. Stakeholders deserve to understand why, not just what — and the team deserves a record to build on.

◐

Research-grounded

I partner with research teams and run lightweight studies myself. Content decisions backed by user data land faster and hold up to scrutiny.

△

Cross-functional fluency

I work upstream with product and design, and downstream with marketing and legal. I translate between these worlds without losing signal.

◇

Measurable impact

I define success metrics before launch and track them after. Content strategy without measurement is just opinion.

03 / Assets

Strategy Artifacts

These are the deliverables that signal senior-level content thinking — artifacts that show your process, not just your prose.

Content Audit

High signal

A systematic inventory with scoring criteria, gap analysis, and recommendations. Shows you can assess at scale before prescribing solutions.

Content Model / Taxonomy

High signal

Structured diagram of content types, relationships, and metadata. Demonstrates IA thinking and understanding of how content is created, stored, and retrieved.

Annotated Journey Map

High signal

User journey with content decisions and rationale layered in. Shows how content decisions serve user needs across an end-to-end experience.

Voice & Tone Framework

High signal

Principles + spectrum + contextual examples. Hiring teams want to see how you codify brand language and make it usable across teams.

Content Brief

Strong signal

Your pre-writing strategic document: goals, audience, scope, success metrics. Proves you think before you type.

Measurement Framework

Strong signal

How you defined success and tracked outcomes. Even qualitative results matter — quote research findings, not just analytics.
